Hi!  I’m Jen Vega, creator and writer of This Is My Momoir.  Thank you so much for being here!  Aside from being a writer, I am the wife of a pretty awesome guy named Dan Vega and the mom of two adorable little girls, Isabella and Juliana.  We are lifelong Clevelanders living in the suburb of Rocky River, Ohio.  I love reading and writing and have a Bachelor of Arts degree in English from Cleveland State University.  I also love to cook, bake, exercise, and travel among other hobbies.  

I started this blog as a way to connect with other women on topics that I know we all find challenging at times.  When I talk with my friends, we all seem to be trying to juggle and balance the same things while also carrying around a lot of insecurity and guilt.  So this is my momoir, a memoir as a mom, of all the things I have learned along my journey.  I hope this can be a place to feel less alone and come together to share, grow, laugh, and make connections that will help to empower us along the way.  

Please feel free to reach out to me on social media, or email me at jen@thisismymomoir.com.  I really hope this can be an interactive community where we can uplift and support each other on our journeys as women.  I truly look forward to hearing from you!
